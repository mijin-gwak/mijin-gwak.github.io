Neural Language Modeling Research

Explore the intricate world of neural language models and their ability to simulate human bilingual sentence processing. This research project was conducted during my research master's, which delves into whether Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) language models exhibit human-like structural adaptation. Discover key concepts such as NP/S-coordination ambiguity, local structural ambiguity, and the effects of a reduced training corpus.
